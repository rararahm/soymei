ABOUT US

Trusted Electrical Distribution Partner Since 2006

Soy Mei Advantage Multi‑Sales, Inc. is a trusted supplier of electrical and mechanical components for commercial, industrial, and residential projects. We specialize in marketing, importing, and distributing a wide range of electrical products, ensuring quality, reliability, and availability for our clients.

With strong partnerships and authorized distributorships of leading global brands such as Cutler‑Hammer, Moeller, General Electric, Fuji, Square D, Telemecanique, Philflex, Columbia, Federal Pacific, and ABB, we deliver dependable solutions backed by technical expertise and excellent customer service.

From plants and warehouses to malls, hotels, hospitals, schools, and residential developments, Soy Mei Advantage continues to power communities and businesses across Metro Manila and beyond.
