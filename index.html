ABOUT US

Trusted Electrical Distribution Partner Since 2006

CH Asia World of Electric Inc. was established in 2006 and registered with Securities and Exchange Commission under Registration No. CS200609065.

Founded with a vision to serve the growing demand for premium electrical solutions, the company has become a trusted supplier of electrical products for commercial, industrial and residential developments.

Through strong partnerships with leading global brands, we continue to provide dependable products, technical expertise and exceptional customer service.
